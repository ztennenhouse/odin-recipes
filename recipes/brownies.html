Brownies Recipe

Delicious Brownies


Ingredients

  • 2 eggs and water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix wet and dry ingredients in separate bowls
  2. Combine both bowls to form batter
  3. Pour into 8x8 inch baking pan
  4. Bake at 325 degrees F for 45 minutes
