Chocolate Chip Cookies


Ingredients

  • 225g butter
  • 110g sugar
  • 275g flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 75g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190C
  2. Cream butter until soft
  3. Add sugar and keep beating until light and fluffy
  4. Sift flour and other ingredients
  5. Roll dough into small balls and place on baking sheet
  6. Bake for 10-12 mins
